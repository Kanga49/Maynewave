Discover Mayne Island

Some places ask you to look.
Mayne invites you to notice.

01

Listen

Birdsong before breakfast. Ferry horns carrying across Active Pass. Waves reaching the shore after the ship has gone.

02

Watch

Eagles overhead, seals in the pass, otters along the shore and the changing light across the islands.

03

Explore

Paddle, cycle, walk the trails, visit the beaches—or simply follow whatever catches your attention.

04

Taste

Island cafés, farm stands, local produce and unhurried meals after a day outside.

05

Seasons

Spring blossoms, summer paddling, autumn colour and winter storms. Mayne is never quite the same island twice.
